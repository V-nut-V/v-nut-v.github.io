Personal Narrative

Hi, my name is Harley Lin. I’m looking for an internship or apprenticeship doing web design and development. Currently, I’m a graphic design student at Wintec, graduating from Cheng Du University in China in 2015, and I will finish all the courses to gain a bachelor’s degree in media arts in July 2018. I’m interested in digital art, such as "3D modelling’, 'motion graphic' and 'web design & development'. I have some work experience which is related to graphic design with a Chinese company called YiZeTang / a New Zealand company Melcome, Email address: harley9387@gmail.com

Study Plan

Next, I plan to get more familiar with javascript by doing more concrete example exercises, based on that, I will learn gulp and babel to improve my workflow. Actually, I learned these before, things like Git, Github, NPM, babel, gulp and some Linux command, but it is too many things have to understand and memorize to me. So that, I will look these over again and try to apply these to work. After that, I want to learn PHP and MySQL to see how they work together, also Ajax, and I think I also need to know how to use WordPress and Bootstrap in the future.
I dream to write some script or even plug-in in some software. The purpose is to expand the ability to realize and help my imagination on design or somewhere else. I like drawing since I was a child, and want to keep it going as a hobby. I believe this can positively affect my future work in many ways.
